## 🔍 **Key Tax Concepts Explained**

### QBI (Qualified Business Income) Deduction
- **What it is**: 20% deduction on business income for pass-through entities
- **BBB Impact**: Lowers phase-out thresholds from $182k to $75k (single filers)
- **Benefit**: More small businesses qualify for the full deduction

### Vehicle Loan Interest Deduction
- **What it is**: New deduction for vehicle loan interest
- **Requirements**: New, U.S.-assembled vehicles purchased 2023-2028
- **Limit**: Up to $10,000 per year in deductible interest

### Asset Financing Changes
- **What changed**: Business interest deduction expanded
- **Technical**: Uses EBIT instead of EBITDA for calculations
- **Impact**: More interest becomes deductible for asset financing
